Radio Advertising / Radio Advertising in India

What is Radio Advertising

When it comes to radio, people are all ears! Radio is a part of our everyday life at work, at leisure, at home, or on the move in the car. Radio serves as an accompaniment to the rhythms of our daily life. When you listen to the radio, you are hearing it on a one-to-one basis as if someone is speaking directly to you. Many people have a close rapport with a radio personality to whom they listen faithfully.

Despite radio being a traditional medium people are still engaged with it on a daily basis. It is due to the growing listenership and loyalty towards the medium that even advertisers flock to it.

The radio industry is expecting to draw better ad volume and ad revenue in 2023. TAM’s study for the industry, which predicts a 25% increase in ad volume in 2022, backs up this optimistic forecast. According to TRAI’s September 2022 quarter report, radio ad revenue increased by 11%.

FM Radio Advertising Agency in India

With the launch of Radio City Bangalore as India’s first private FM radio station, FM Radio Advertising in India started on July 3, 2001. All India Radio popularly known as AIR was the government Radio agency that used to broadcast Radio content and Radio ads in India. All over the world FM Radio advertising present almost 10% of the total marketing spends but in India Radio Advertising this account is less than 5% of the total marketing spend. Popular FM stations in India are Radio Mirchi, Radio One, Red FM, Radio Fever, Hello FM, Big FM, Radio City, Radio Mango, Surya FM, Rangila FM, Radio Nasha, AIR FM Gold, Vividh Bharti, AIR FM Rainbow, Magic FM, Best FM, Sarthak FM, and Magic 106.4 FM. There are approximately more than 80 FM stations across cities in India. Large FM Radio markets are Bangalore, Delhi, Chennai, Kolkata and Mumbai.

Types of Radio Advertising

Time Check

Time checks are those short lines within ads announcing the time to the listener. They play a dual role: informing listeners about the current time, while subtly telling them about the brand.

Sponsorship by Radio

Brands can sponsor specific radio shows or programs. With such an association, brands can align themselves with content that might be relevant for that target audience. It’s a type of advertisement where credentials are also gained because the brand is associated with popular shows.

RJ Mentions

Radio jockeys (RJs) can talk about products or services while delivering their programs. It lends it an added human element to the ad. The RJ endorsement can, thus, increase trust and possibly have an enhanced consumer relationship, thus making the ad much more impactful.
